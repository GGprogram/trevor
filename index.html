Trevor Commands

Music Commands

/join 'Trevor joins the voice channel,  /j'

/play 'Trevor plays a song, "/play songname"  /p'

/queue 'Add a song to the queue, "/queue songname" /q'

/pause 'Pause the listening party, /pa or /pau' 

/resume 'Resume listening to paused music, /r or /res'

/stop 'Stop listening and clear the queue,  /s'

/next 'Skip to the next song in the queue, /n or /skip' 

/leave 'Trevor leaves the current channel, /l or /lea'

Admin Commands

/roleadd 'React for role, /roleadd #channel :emoji: @role'

/checkperms 'Check if you're an admin'

/kick 'Kick a member from the server'

/perma 'Permanently ban with reason, /perma @mention reason'

/del5 'Delete last 5 messages '

/del10 'Delete last 10 messages'
